AROUND THE WORLD

  • Fire risks

    Agriculture-related fires are fires that occur in rural or agricultural areas and can have multiple causes and consequences. They can affect crops, pastures, agricultural facilities, equipment and even wildlife.

    Our goal is to provide valuable knowledge and practical resources to help you be prepared and safe in the face of the challenges fires can pose. Browse our website for detailed and reliable information on how to deal with this threat calmly and effectively.

  • Rainfall indices

    Rain plays a vital role in agriculture, directly influencing harvest success and agricultural productivity. This site explores the relationship between rainfall and agriculture, highlighting its significant impact.

    Our mission is to provide valuable information to farmers, students, researchers and agriculture enthusiasts, helping everyone understand how rainfall affects food production and the importance of sustainable agricultural practices in an ever-changing world. Explore our website to learn more about this crucial topic for global agriculture.
